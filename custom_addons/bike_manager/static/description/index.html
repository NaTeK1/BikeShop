Bike Shop Management System

Overview

A comprehensive Odoo module for managing bike shops offering both sales and rental services. This module provides complete functionality for product catalog management, customer tracking, sales orders, rental contracts, and reporting.

Product Catalog

  • Hierarchical product categories
  • Bike models/templates
  • Individual products (bikes, accessories, parts)
  • Stock management with availability tracking

Sales Management

  • Customer order processing
  • Automatic stock updates
  • Multiple payment methods
  • Order workflow management

Rental Management

  • Flexible rental contracts
  • Hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly pricing
  • Availability checking
  • Deposit and late fee management
  • Condition tracking

Customer Management

  • Complete customer profiles
  • Purchase and rental history
  • Statistical overview
  • Activity tracking

Reporting

  • Sales by product
  • Bike occupancy rates
  • Rental revenue tracking
  • Customer statistics

Key Features

  • Full Odoo 19.0 Community compatibility
  • Complete MVC architecture
  • Comprehensive data validation
  • Demo data included
  • Multi-view support (list, form, kanban, calendar)
  • State-based workflows
  • Professional UI/UX
  • Documented and maintainable code
